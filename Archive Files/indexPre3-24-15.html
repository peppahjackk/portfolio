Video & Web Design

      Thanks for taking the time to visit my page, let me tell you a little about myself. I am in my early 20s and have a passion for the marriage of web and video. Since watching my first YouTube video I knew the web would be a special platform for accessible video content. Since the summer of 2014 I have been working part-time at MLB.com as a Gamenight Cutter for Major League Baseball games and a Commercial Logger for ESPN3 streams. From our office in Manhattan I coordinate with on site production teams to ensure the highest quality sport streams on any device through the WatchEspn app and MLBTV.
      In between shifts I am honing my web design skills through personal and freelance projects. While earning my Bachelor's degree in Radio/Television/Film, I fulfilled and exceeded the requirements for a concentration in New Media. This concentration focused on bringing various forms of media together and weaving them into the rapidly changing web. When I'm not working I enjoy playing golf, playing games on my PC, and eating various types of pie (mainly the pizza type).
